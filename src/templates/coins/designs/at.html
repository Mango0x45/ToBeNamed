{{ _("1-, 2-, and 5 Euro Cent Coins") }}

{% trans %} These coins feature various flowers from the Alps. They were chosen to symbolize the role of Austria in the development of EU environmental policy. The coins also feature the Austrian flag underneath their respective flowers. {% endtrans %}

{{ _("10-, 20-, and 50 Euro Cent Coins") }}

{% trans %} These coins feature the different kinds of architecture in Austria. The buildings depicted are the St. Stephen’s Cathedral, Belvedere Palace, and Secession Hall. Respectively they represent the Gothic-, Baroque-, and Art Nouveau- architectural styles. {% endtrans %}

{{ _("1- and 2 Euro Coins") }}

{% trans %} These coins feature the portraits of famous Austrians. The 1 euro coin features Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart, the famous Austrian composer from Salzburg. His signature is also shown on his shoulder. The 2 euro coin features Bertha von Suttner, an Austrian winner of the Nobel Peace Prize ‘for her audacity to oppose the horrors of war’. {% endtrans %}
