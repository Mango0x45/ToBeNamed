{{ _("The originals") }}

{% trans %} The 2002 series of banknotes follow the theme of ‘The Ages and Styles of Europe’, featuring a building or window on the front with a bridge on the back in different European architectural styles. All buildings and bridges featured on the banknotes are purely fictional. {% endtrans %}

{% trans %} The 5-, 10-, and 20 euro banknotes all feature a security strip on the right hand side as an anti-counterfieting measure while the 50-, 100-, 200-, and 500 euro banknotes feature a blob rather than a full strip. All the notes also feature the stars of Europe on the front and back of the notes as well as the flag of Europe in the top left. {% endtrans %}
