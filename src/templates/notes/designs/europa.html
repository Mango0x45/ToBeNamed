{{ _("What a creative name") }}

{% trans %} The Europa series of banknotes were a redesign of the original 2002 series of banknotes aimed at improving security. This series notably lacks the 500 euro banknote that was introduced in the 2002 series. {% endtrans %}

{% trans %} Unlike the previous series, in the Europa series all banknotes featured a full security strip. The text on the banknotes were also expanded to support the languages of newer European Union members, including text in the cyrillic script. As per the series name, all banknotes feature a watermark of the goddess Europa, as well as an image of Europa in the window of the security strip. {% endtrans %}

{% trans %} The 100- and 200 euro banknotes were also made slightly shorter, at 77 millimeters in length as opposed to the previous 82 millimeters. This gives them the same width as the 50 euro banknote. {% endtrans %}
